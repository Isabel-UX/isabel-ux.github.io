E-commerce redesign

This proposal focuses on improving the experience of users who browse and shop their groceries through Mercadona's website. The full report includes the neccesary research, architecture, sketches, wireframes and final high definition designs of several screens of the website.



This project, redesign of Mercadona's online shop, gives an answer to one of the most common problems in the market, the updating of the competitive framework and the improvement of the usability. Its elaboration starts from the need to evaluate the current situation of the platform, to later investigate and know which are the users' requirements and the contexts of use; and thus create a design solution that, with the participation of the users, results in an improvement in the usability and understanding of the site.


Thus, the importance of research and User Centred Design. A compendium of methodologies and ways of working that put the user in the centre of all phases of the design process, in which it participates or in which it is directly related.



General view


The project implementation phases were :
1. Analysis phase
2. Research phase
3. Design phase
4. Evaluation phase



1. Analysis


Heuristic evaluation of the brand's available online stores through an evaluation rubric.



2. Research


Semi-structured Interviews, Surveys, Personae + Scenario Archetyping and Benchmarking.




3. Design


Card Sorting tests for the construction of the Information Architecture. Open CS + Closed CS.



4. Evaluation


User test on the designed solution and relative satisfaction survey.
