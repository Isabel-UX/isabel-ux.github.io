Web redesign projects

No matter what you do in life, you will no stop learning new things everyday. Design is no exception. Techonolgies with which we work are constantly evolving and trends are always changing. For that matter, reviewing past design solutions it is a natural part of the process.



In this post I would like to share two passion projects where I redesign two different websites as part of my training as a ux / ui designer.


  • 1. Long Life Family Study Webpage
  • 2. ATI Aquaristik Webpage

1. Long Life Family Study Webpage


- Scientific study of healthy aging.
- Need to advertise the study to other scientists.
- Have an impressive amount of content but it is lacking organization and aesthetics.
- The presentation it is not apealing.
- Need to advertise the possibility of joining the study.



2. ATI Aquaristik Webpage


- Elegant design. Expert users (clients) are expected.
- Not a usual e-commerce. ATI is a community and retailer of high quality products.
- Avoid excessive decorations. Maintain a vertial rhythm so the design can breathe.
- Advertise new arrivals.
- The design must respond to the complete user experience.
